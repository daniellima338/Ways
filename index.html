Experience

expand your competences

We live in a world that is constantly changing, being presented with complex, multicultural issues almost on a daily basis. WAYS helps strengthen young peoples intercultural and communication skills, as well as many other much needed competences.

Extend Your Network

Create new relations with people from all over the world, and form friendships that will last for the rest of your life. Since 2015, WAYS have provided intercultural learning experiences for hundreds of young people in more than 30 countries.

Explore The World

WAYS has several years of experience with international youth projects, both in Europe, the Middle East & North Africa. With us, you won’t just be travelling, you will also gain whole new perspectives that would not be possible as a regular tourist or backpacker.
